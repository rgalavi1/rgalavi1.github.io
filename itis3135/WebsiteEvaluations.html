Website Evaluations

Protest

Protest is a sportswear company in Holland founded in 1993. Founded by a small group of snowboarders.

  • C:The opacity of the homepage image is balanced with the white text. The white text throughout the page is also balanced with the white backgorund.
  • R:The font size and style throughout the page is consistant. The bold and big text repeats to establish key points in the page. The smaller text is also consistant.
  • A:There is alignment with the pictures and description of the pictures.
  • P:Spacing between elements helps the user navigate the page. Products and their descriptions are grouped together.

LingsCars

LingsCar is a car leasing company in the United Kingdom.

  • C:There is no balance of color throughout the page. The contrast does not help visibility and makes it harder to read.
  • R:There is no repetition in the font styles and sizes. It is hard to understand what the key points are for the user.
  • A:Elements look like they were blindly slapped on the page. There is no consistant alignment with graphics and text.
  • P:There is no space between elements to let the user know that they are not related.
