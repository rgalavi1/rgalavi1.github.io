Reviving Goat Inc.

Reviving Goat Inc was founded by Daniel Terminator. As a young boy Daniel always wanted a goat. He would see various goats on his walk to school, which would be miles of hiking. As he became older his love for goats grew until finally he was able to afford and own his own goat. His company dedicates their efforts to providing less fortunate goat enthusiastic children the ability to adopt their own goat. No goat is too far because Reviving Goat Inc. ships goats worldwide and will make sure each child gets their own goat. A smile is guaranteed.

Here at Reviving Goat Inc. we dedicate our time and effort into ensuring that every goat goes to a nice family. We know what it's like to want a goat, so as a thank you for your first goat we will throw in a goats fur coat. Daniel Terminator has traveled the world meeting people just like him. He enjoys seeing the smiles on kids' faces when they see their first goat right in front of them. So we would like to throw in a limited edition signed copy of Daniel Terminator's unreleased book: The Terminator. Thank you for being part of our family, and we hope to see you again soon.
